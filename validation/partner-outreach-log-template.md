## Evidence Permission Checklist

Before using any feedback in an application, confirm:

- The person agrees the quote or summary may be used.
- The organization name can be used, or the evidence must be anonymized.
- The role/title can be used, or it must be generalized.
- No confidential workload, customer, prompt, infrastructure, or security details are included.
- The final redacted quote has been reviewed if required.
